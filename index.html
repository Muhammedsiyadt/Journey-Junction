Explore Wayanad’s Hidden Gems

Meppadi

A scenic hill village known for tea estates, waterfalls, and misty viewpoints like Soochipara and Kanthanpara.

Banasura

Home to India’s largest earthen dam, surrounded by mountains and ideal for boating and trekking adventures.

Vythiri

A lush rainforest getaway famous for its resorts, hanging bridges, and tranquil streams. Perfect for couples.

Kuruva Island

An eco-tourism spot with over 900 acres of forested river islands. Great for bamboo rafting and nature trails.

900 Kandi

A secluded forest paradise known for its off-road adventure, glass bridge, and breathtaking panoramic views.
